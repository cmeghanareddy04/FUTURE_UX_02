----------------------------------------------------------------
  ABOUT THIS PROJECT
----------------------------------------------------------------
SmileBright Dental Appointment Booking App is a complete
mobile UI/UX design for a local dental clinic. The app allows
patients to browse services, choose a specialist, pick a date
and time, and confirm bookings in under 60 seconds — with
instant WhatsApp confirmation.

----------------------------------------------------------------
  APP SCREENS
----------------------------------------------------------------

  01. Splash Screen
      - Brand logo and tagline
      - Get Started and Sign In CTAs

  02. Onboarding — Slide 1
      - "Book in 60 seconds"
      - Progress dots and Skip option

  03. Onboarding — Slide 2
      - "Choose your specialist"
      - Progress dots and Skip option

  04. Onboarding — Slide 3
      - "Instant confirmation"
      - Create Account and Sign In CTAs

  05. Login / Sign Up
      - Phone number and password
      - Google and WhatsApp social login
      - Forgot password link

  06. Home Dashboard
      - Personalized greeting
      - Upcoming appointment card
      - Quick book grid (2 columns)
      - Specialist scroll
      - Promo banner

  07. Services Screen
      - Filter pills (All, General, Cosmetic, Ortho)
      - Service cards with price and duration
      - Tap to proceed flow

  08. Specialist Selection
      - Doctor profile cards
      - Star ratings and experience
      - Availability status
      - Continue CTA

  09. Date and Time Booking
      - Full month calendar
      - Dot indicators for available slots
      - Time slot grid (Available / Selected / Full)
      - Booking summary bar

  10. Booking Confirmation
      - Full booking summary
      - Itemized payment breakdown
      - UPI, Card, Net banking, Pay at clinic
      - Secure payment note

  11. Success Screen
      - Booking confirmed check
      - Full appointment receipt
      - Unique booking ID
      - Add to calendar link

----------------------------------------------------------------
  KEY FEATURES
----------------------------------------------------------------

  [1]  Book in under 60 seconds from open to confirmed
  [2]  Real-time slot availability with dot indicators
  [3]  Specialist profiles with ratings and experience
  [4]  Multiple payment modes (UPI, Card, Cash)
  [5]  WhatsApp confirmation within 30 minutes
  [6]  Auto reminders 24 hours before appointment
  [7]  Free cancellation up to 2 hours before visit
  [8]  Sticky bottom CTA for one-tap access on mobile
  [9]  Social login via Google and WhatsApp
  [10] Success screen with booking ID and calendar link
